Public Safety DAS

Your property's safety isn't solely dependent on having up-to-date access control and CCTV systems. In emergencies, it's crucial to ensure first responders can communicate seamlessly, despite structural or physical barriers. A Public Safety DAS can help bridge these safety gaps in your facility.

Public Safety/First Responder DAS (Distributed Antenna System)

It’s a system that’s primarily used to ensure that emergency or first responders can have access to seamless, uninterrupted wireless communication while they’re inside buildings, garages, stairwells, and tunnels.

Relevance to Safety

Radio communications have proven to be a critical lifeline for first responders. However, environmental and structural obstacles can compromise or block wireless signals, reducing the effectiveness and safety of fire department personnel and police officers during emergencies.

Advantage

Our team of Engineers and Installation Technicians can design and deploy a tailor-fit DAS solution for your property. Our customized systems provide emergency responders access to seamless and uninterrupted wireless communication.
